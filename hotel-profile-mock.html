Inside Scoop

  • Sexy, upscale art deco oceanfront hotel designed by Philippe Starck in collobaration with Lenny Kravitz.
  • Located in the heart of South Beach, explore Miami nightlife or create your own pool party on property.
  • Home to Hyde Beach, a day club with over 8,000 square feet of pool, beach and indoor/outdoor space.
  • The Bazar by Chef José Andrés, features a vibrant mix of sophisticated cuisine and a playful indoor-outdoor environment.
  • Katsuya unleashes culinary artistry with a menu of innovative sushi and robata dishes uniquely adapted for the American palate

Things to know

  • Daily Resort Fee of $30 per day, per room (plus tax) includes Wi-Fi access resort-wide, two beach chairs, and Fitness Center access.
  • Sophisticated rooms are small (starting at 255 square feet) yet luxurious.
  • Small pets under 15lbs are allowed with a nonrefundable fee of $150.00 dollars per pet per day.
  • Originally built in 1939, its rooftop design depicts a lantern reaching toward the sky, and is the tallest art deco building in South Beach.
  • Valet parking fee: USD 44.00 per night.
  • Pet fee: USD 150.00 per pet, per night.
  • Resort fee: $30 plus tax per room, per night.
